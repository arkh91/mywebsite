Grading:

  • Assignments

    • There will be 6 assignments worth a maximum of 40 percent of your final grade.
    • Prolog Assignment: 10%
    • Scheme Assignment: 10%
    • Other assignments worth 20% in total.

    All assignments must be submitted on paper.
    Links  to the assignments will appear in the above schedule, when they become available.

    Each assignment will have a specified due date. Assignments handed in after the due date will be subject to a penalty of 10% per day, 20% per Saturday/Sunday, up to 50% off, after which it will receive a grade of 0.

    Participation in the lab is mandatory.
     

  • Project 15% Presentation 5%
  • Midterm
    • There will be one midterm on Oct 9. It will count for a maximum of 15 percent of your final grade.
     
  • Final Exam
    • In addition, there will be a final exam worth a minimum of 25 percent. The mark on section one of the final exam, the section that covers the same material as the midterm, can replace the midterm mark, if it is to the student's advantage.

     

    Letter grades will be awarded as follows:

    B+ >= 75.0 B >= 70.0 B- >= 65.0
    C+ >= 60.0 C >= 55.0 D   >= 50.0
